Our Culinary Journey

More than just food. It's an experience.

Burgers

The burger journey began in the late 19th century, evolving from a simple steak to a global icon. We honor this history by grilling our patties to perfection, ensuring every bite is a celebration of flavor.

Classic Veg Burger

A timeless classic. Our vegetable patty is crafted with farm-fresh greens and aromatic spices, crisped to golden perfection. It’s the taste of nostalgia in every bite.

Cheese Burger

Legend says the cheeseburger was created by accident in the 1920s. We recreate that magic by melting a thick slice of premium cheese over a hot patty, creating a gooey, savory masterpiece.

Paneer Burger

An Indian twist on the Western classic. We marinate fresh paneer in a secret yogurt-spice blend and grill it until it's smoky and tender. A royal treat for vegetarians.

Crispy Special Burger

Our Chef's Masterpiece. We developed a double-coating technique to ensure maximum crunch using secret herbs. It's not just a burger; it's a texture explosion.

Chowmein

Originating from China and loved by India. Our Chowmein is wok-tossed at high flames to capture the 'Wok Hei' - the breath of the wok, giving it that distinct smoky flavor.

Veg Chowmein

Hand-pulled noodles tossed with crunchy cabbage, carrots, and bell peppers. Simple, authentic, and full of that street-style zest.

Paneer Chowmein

Soft cubes of fresh paneer meet spicy noodles. The creamy texture of paneer balances the spicy soy-garlic sauce perfectly.

Special Chowmein

A carnival of flavors! Loaded with exotic vegetables and tossed in our secret spicy sauce blend. It's a party on your palate.

Rice & Manchurian

A fusion of bold flavors. Our Manchurian balls are handcrafted daily, and our rice is stir-fried to fluffy perfection.

Veg Manchurian

Finely chopped veggies formed into golden dumplings, simmered in a dark, rich soy-garlic gravy. A glorious invention of Indo-Chinese cuisine.

Paneer Manchurian

Fried paneer cubes tossed in a tangy Manchurian sauce. The crispy exterior and soft interior of the paneer create a delightful contrast.

Veg Fried Rice

Long grain basmati rice, wok-tossed with fresh veggies and a hint of black pepper. Light, aromatic, and delicious.

Paneer Fried Rice

Our classic fried rice upgraded with golden-fried paneer bits. A wholesome meal that fills you up with joy.

Special Fried Rice

The King of Rice dishes. We add cashews, raisins, and exotic veggies to make this a royal feast fit for a king.

Starters

Crispy Honey Potato

Crispy potato fingers glazed with sweet honey and spicy chili sesame sauce. A perfectly balanced sweet and spicy addiction.

Chef's Special Honey Potato

Our chef's secret version with extra crunch and a hint of ginger, topped with roasted sesame seeds.

Classic Paneer Tikka

Born from the Tandoors of Punjab. Paneer marinated in yogurt and spices, roasted until charred. Smoky and succulent.

Tandoori Paneer Tikka

Extra smoky, extra spicy. Marinated overnight to let the flavor penetrate deep into the paneer.

Veg Spring Roll

A crispy wrapper filled with a savory vegetable mix, fried to a golden hue. The perfect crunch in every bite.

Paneer Spring Roll

The crispy skin outside meets soft, spiced grated paneer inside. A texture combination made in heaven.

MUST TRY

Dahi Bda

A Tradition of Coolness. Soft lentil dumplings soaked in creamy, chilled yogurt, topped with tangy tamarind chutney and roasted cumin. The ultimate comfort food.
